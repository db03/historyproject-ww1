Hydrogen Sulfide

First used by: the Allies (Britain)

This gas was not ideal for war - being less powerful than others - but it was used twice in 1916 when other gases were in short supply. It's a colourless gas but has a very strong smell of eggs.

Effects of Hydrogen Sulfide:

Death - it's poisonous.
