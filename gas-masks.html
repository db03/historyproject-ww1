Gas Masks

Gas masks were created to prevent toxic gases being able to get into someone's system. They obviously couldn't stop gases from damaging soldiers' skin if they were capable of doing so. The first thing to be created like this was when someone recommended urinating on a cloth and putting it to your mouth in case of a gas attack. Chlorine gas would react with the urea in urine and would stop it from damaging your body. As the war went on, gas masks developed into different things and had to change to deal with different kinds of gases.
