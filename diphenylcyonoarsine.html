Diphenylcyonoarsine

First used by: the Central Powers (Germany)

Similar to Diphenylchlorarsine, but more powerful, replacing it as filling for "blue cross" shells.

Effects of Diphenylcyonoarsine:

Extreme vomiting, sneezing and sometimes headaches if adminstered at the right concentration.
